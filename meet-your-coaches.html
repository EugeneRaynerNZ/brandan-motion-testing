Monique

Hey ladies! I'm Monique, one of the owners and qualified coaches here at Motion Fitness.

When we work together, it's not just about hitting a goal—it's about building confidence, strength, and a lifestyle approach to fitness that truly works for you. My method is simple: no stress, no guilt, no unrealistic routines. Just a balanced, sustainable way to feel your best.

I know how overwhelming it can be to juggle workouts, nutrition, and everyday life. That's why I create personalized plans that fit into your schedule, making it easier to stay consistent and see real progress. But more than that, I want you to understand why you're doing what you do. Through education, habit-building, and a focus on long-term success, you'll gain the knowledge and confidence to maintain your results for life.

Whether you want to feel amazing in a bikini, keep up with your kids, or stay active for years to come, I'm here to guide and support you every step of the way to reach your goals. Together we can create a strong, confident, and happy you—inside and out!
